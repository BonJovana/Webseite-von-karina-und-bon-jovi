About me

My name is Jovana, I’m an Artist and currently studying
Media Design in the University of applied Sciences in Weingarten.


During my Highschool years I spent a lot of time perfecting my digital paint
and drawing skills.
These days my time is spent painting, designing, prototyping, animating and
sculpting. Aside from studying in Uni, I also work on commissions from time
to time.
Out of Uni you can find me singing, painting or creating new Character
Concepts to build into my ever growing Fantasy Comic I’ve been working on
for years.
